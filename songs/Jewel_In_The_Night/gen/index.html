Jewel in the Night

By Chris Hadfield

The beuaty of Earth from above, and a re-affirmation of Christmas

This song was written and recorded aboard the international space station. An audio recording with photos is available.

(Yes, this is the same Chris Hadfield who did the famous Space Oddity Cover.)
