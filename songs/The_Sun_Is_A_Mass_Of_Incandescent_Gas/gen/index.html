The Sun Is A Mass Of Incandescent Gas

By They Might Be Giants (Or Glazer & Evans)

A mostly accurate ode to the sun

An outside song, original music video here and lyrics here

We use the TMBG version, but it’s based on an older, very similar song by Tom Glazer and Dottie Evans. How much credit the latter should get is debatable. They’re kind of the same song, but only kind of.
