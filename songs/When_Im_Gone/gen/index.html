The Mary Ellen Spider

Tune by Stan Rogers, Lyrics by Bob Blue

A metaphor of persistance

This is a parody of Mary Ellen Carter, a bit sillier and with even less subtext. Various versions of this exist, with the earliest I’m aware of being Tom Smith’s version on youtube. Sometimes this is called Eensie Weensie Spider, but there’s a different song sometimes using that title.
