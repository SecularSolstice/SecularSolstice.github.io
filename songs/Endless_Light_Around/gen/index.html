Endless Light Around Me

By Raymond Arnold

The second part of a song of hope, generations, stories and infinite possibilities

This is basically the last two verses of Endless Light. I split it just so that it would stop fighting with Brighter than Today for the Dawn slot, of which there is really only one.

The instrumental opening gives us a chance to pull the first half into our minds. We can use the motif we established there, as well as repeating the ending melody. Between those, (which are available on one page) we should be able to pick up where we left off pretty smoothly.
