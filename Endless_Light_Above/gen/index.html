Endless Light Above Me

By Raymond Arnold

The first part of a song of hope, generations, stories and infinite possibilities

This is basically the first verse of Endless Light. I split it just so that it would stop fighting with Brighter than Today for the Dawn slot, of which there is really only one.

Because this is a split song, we want to recognize its reappearance, even though we've heard a lot of other themes in between. And we want that recognition to come from the instrumental lead-in, before the singer enters. To do that, let us use this song to establish a piano motif. This especially goes under the slow descents ("Endless lights", "Untold stories", etc.)
